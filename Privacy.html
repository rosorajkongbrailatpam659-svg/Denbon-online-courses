Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal data.

Information We Collect
We collect information you voluntarily provide during course enrollment or while using website features. This includes your name, email address, location, payment details, and any other data you submit. We also automatically collect technical data sent by your browser during site visits, such as IP address, browser type, device information, and access times.

Use of Personal Data
The personal data we collect allows us to deliver customized services, process payments, communicate with you about orders and offerings, update you on course progress, post relevant forums content, and display appropriate advertisements. It also assists with website administration, security, and research.

Data Sharing and Disclosure
We will not sell or rent your personal data to outside parties. It may be shared internally or with trusted third-party service partners in order to facilitate orders, hosting servers, analytics platforms, or other essential activities related to our services. It can also be disclosed to comply with legal requirements.

Data Retention
We retain personal data for active users as long as your account is open. For inactive users, data is archived after varying periods depending on its purpose, in accordance with regulations and our internal policies.

Your Rights
You have the right under GDPR to access, correct, delete, or limit our usage of your personal data. Please contact us to enact those rights. You also have the right to opt-out of marketing communications.

Security
We follow industry standards and implement robust measures to protect the security and integrity of information under our control. However, no data transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Updates
We reserve the right to occasionally update this privacy policy as website practices or privacy regulations evolve. You should refer back regularly for the latest version.

Contact Us
Please direct any questions, comments, or requests related to this privacy policy to our Data Protection Officer.
